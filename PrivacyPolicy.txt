 Privacy Policy for Social Improvement Society :

 - At Social Improvement Society, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information provided by our members, donors, and visitors.

 1. Information We Collect :

 - We may collect the following types of informations,

 Personal Information : 
	When you sign up for membership, make a donation, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details.

 Usage Data : 
	We may collect information about your interactions with our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

 2. How We Use Your Information :

	 We use the information we collect for the following purposes,

 - To process membership registrations and donations
 - To communicate with you regarding updates, events, and initiatives
 - To improve our programs and services
 - To maintain the security and functionality of our website

 3. Sharing Your Information :
	We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with trusted partners or service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our activities, or supporting our mission. These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential.

 4. Data Security :
	We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please understand that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

 5. Donations and Membership Fees :
	For those making donations or paying membership fees, please note that all contributions are processed securely through trusted payment processors. We do not store credit card or payment information on our systems.

 6. Your Rights :
	You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections or deletions. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any concerns about your personal data, please contact us.

 7. Updates to This Privacy Policy :
	We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

 Thank you for your support and trust in Social Improvement Society. Your privacy is important to us.
